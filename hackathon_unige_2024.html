Swiss Cosmo Hackathon 2024

Bridging data, people, and coffee.

About

In which ways can we use data from multiple cosmological surveys to improve our understanding of fundamental physics?

Are there new observables we are missing?

Which techniques or ideas are you most excited about?


This hackathon will bring people with different expertise to work on projects linked to observational cosmology.


Time: 14:15 - 19:00

Date: Friday, 6th of December 2024

Address: Sciences II room 223, Université de Genève / Sciences II et III, Quai Ernest-Ansermet 30, 1205 Genève

Format

We will be split in teams:

  • Each team will start from a few data sets, and will decide what can be done with them (the opposite can also be done).
  • You will need to define your what, how, and why.
  • You will have freedom to work on your ideas.
  • At the end of the day, participants will present their results to the other teams.
  • To help you going on, we will have refreshments.
