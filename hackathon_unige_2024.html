Swiss Cosmo Hackathon 2024

Join us to explore the universe through data and innovation!

About

The hackathon will bring people with different expertise to work on projects linked to observational cosmology.


Time: 14:15 - 19:00

Date: Friday, 6th of December 2024

Address: Sciences II room 223, Université de Genève / Sciences II et III, Quai Ernest-Ansermet 30, 1205 Genève

Goals

Our primary goals include:

  • Encouraging collaboration among enthusiasts and experts in cosmology.
  • Developing innovative solutions to analyze cosmological data.
  • Exploring new theories and methods to understand the universe.

Data Products

Participants will have access to:

  • Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) datasets.
  • Galaxy redshift surveys.
  • Gravitational wave data.
  • Simulations of large-scale structures in the universe.
